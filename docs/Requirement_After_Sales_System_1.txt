B. (Automation)

         1. Cycle Ł Auto fill
                         >= 21:00:00 maka "Cycle 1 (21.00 Ł 15.00)"
               <= 15:00:00 maka "Cycle 1 (21.00 Ł 15.00)"
               >=15:00:00 maka Cycle 2 "(15.01 Ł 20.59)"
               <=21:00:00 maka "Cycle 2 (15.01 Ł 20.59)"
2. SKU Ł auto fill product name & qty
3. Sub Cases Ł autofill

                    Bad Quality Product atau Expired = "BRAND" (no editable)
                    Misunderstanding of the product= "CUSTOMER"
                    OOS= "KAE"
                    Promotion = "PROMO"
                    Change Mind = "CUSTOMER"
                    di luar category tersebut isi manual dg pilihan "Cause/By"

4. Status Ł auto fill dari last step

               Claim Receive (10x shipping fee), Claim Receive (Full), Complaint Canceled by
                   buyer/No Respons, Product has been delivered (Late Delivery), Refund has been
                   transferred by finance (SPF), Return Refund (Full), Return Refund (Partial), Seller
                   Win, The replacement product has been received by the buyer, or Return follow-
                   up (No further action) = Solved

               Claim Reject = Whitelist
               Selain itu = Pending

5. Priority

              Claim Receive (10x shipping fee), Claim Receive (Full), Complaint Canceled by
                 buyer/No Respons, Product has been delivered (Late Delivery), Refund has
                 been transferred by finance (SPF), Return Refund (Full), Return Refund
                 (Partial), atau Seller Win maka Cool

              Claim Reject = Mines
              Analysis MP (Non Late Delivery), Follow Up After Sales Team, atau Follow Up

                 WH = P1
              On the way return & plan banding, Follow Up KAE to KAM, atau Follow Up

                 KAE/Brand = P2
              FU Courier (MP Non aktif), On the way return & plan refund, Pending return

                 & plan banding, Pending return & plan refund, Pending RGO & plan refund,
                 atau Waiting Data From Customer = P3
              On the way return & plan replace atau Pending return & plan replace maka
                 P4
              Analysis MP (Late Delivery) or Return not authorize = P5
              Kingdee Processing (Waiting AWB for replacement product), Refund
                 processing by finance (SPF), atau Replacement product on the way = P6
              Waiting Claim atau Waiting Money Receive maka P7

6. SLA Ł Auto fill dari tanggal complaint (-) tanggal berjalan, berhenti di tanggal updatejika
    statusnya solved
7. Category Customer Ł Autofill dari username
                    Jika username tersebut complaint pertama kali, maka kosong
                    Jika customer complaint ke dua kali maka "Customer ini complaint ke 2",
                    Jika customernya complaint ketiga kali, maka labelnya ""Customer ini
                        complaint ke 3x"
                    Seterusnya ngikuti jumlah input username dg nama yang sama

8. Riwayat OOS
                    Jika nomor order di menu complaint merupakan duplicate dg no order yang
                        diinput KAE di data OOS, maka beri label "Ada Riwayat OOS"
                    Jika tidak ada biarkan kosong
